## CFL operations
CFL are Kleene Algebras
which means operation include $\circ,\cup,*$ 

$A=(\{ S_{A} \},\{ a \},R_{A},S_{A})$ 
$B=(\{ S_{B} \},\{ b \},R_{B},S_{B})$ 

Union
$A\cup B=(\{ S,S_{A},S_{B} \},\{ a,b \},\{ R,R_{A},R_{B} \},S)$ 
$R:S\to S_{A}|S_{B}$ 

Concatenation
$A\circ B=(\{ S,S_{A},S_{B} \},\{ ab \},\{ R,R_{A},R_{B} \},S)$ 
$R:S\to S_{a}S_{b}$ 

Star
$A*=(\{ S,S_{A} \},\{ a \},\{ R,R_{A} \},S)$ 
$R:S \to S_{A}S|\epsilon$
